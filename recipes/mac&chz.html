Mac and Cheese

ingredients

  • Pasta from box
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • cheese powder from box

Recipe

  1. Bring several cups of water to a boil
  2. Add the macaroni to the boiling water, cook unitl macaroni is soft
  3. strain macaroni and place back into the pot
  4. add butter, milk and cheese sauce to the pot, stir until cheese becomes a sauce and covers macaroni evenly.
  5. Move to a bowl or plate or eat from the pan and Enjoy!
