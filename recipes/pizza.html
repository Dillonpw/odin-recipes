Pizza

A timeless, universally enjoyed food. Here in its purest and simplest form: Dough, Sause, Cheese.

ingredients

  • 1 pre- made pizza dough
  • Tomato/Pizza sauce
  • Cheese

Recipe

  1. Lay out the dough of your choice, top with a light coating, or heavy depending on your taste buds, of your preferred sauce.
  2. Sprinkle your favorite cheeses on top unitl sauce is mostly covered.
  3. Bake in the oven at 400 degrees untildough is firm on the bottom and cheese is golden brown.
  4. Slice and Enjoy!
