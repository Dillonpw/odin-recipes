Sun Dried Tomato Pasta

.

ingredients

  • 1lb Pasta of your choice
  • 8oz Sun dried tomatoes
  • Diced chicken breast
  • 1 cup Spinach
  • 4 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 medium shallot, chopped
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tsp dijon mustard
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Recipe

  1. Drain 3 Tbps of oil from the sundried tomatoes into a large skillet.
  2. Set the skillet over medium-high heat, add chicken, 3 tsp italian seasoning then cook until golden brown, add 1/4 cup parmesan and cook for another minute.
  3. Remove chicken, add some butter, shallot, garlic and 1 tsp italian seasoning, cook unitl fragrant.
  4. Add 3.5 cups water, bring to a boil, then add pasta until Al Dente (~ 8 mins). Stir in cream, mustard, paarmesain, spinach and tomatoes, add the chicken and stir until spinach is wilted.
  5. Top with lemon juice and enjoy!
